    Aesthetics

    Our skin is our largest organ.

    Unlike most of our other organs, the skin is constantly exposed; taking a beating from the sun, wind, and the environment. The skin consists of three layers: the epidermis, the dermis, and the fat layer. We are constantly shedding our skin cells — we shed our entire skin about once a month! The process is called exfoliation.

    If you want healthy, glowing skin, book a consultation today!!!
    Book a consultation

    Chemical peels simply give the process of exfoliation a boost. Peels remove layers of skin so that younger, healthier skin cells can show themselves.

    In the process, age spots, acne scars, fine lines, dead skin cells, hyperpigmentation, wrinkles, and other imperfections are peeled away. The purpose of a peel is to erase imperfections and improve skin tone and texture.

    Chemical peels can be used to:

    • Reduce fine lines
    • Improve skin texture
    • Treat face/back acne
    • Improve signs of premature aging - sun damage and wrinkles
    • Even out pigmentation irregularities including freckles, melasma, and age spots

    Bio-identical Hormone Therapy (BHRT)

    Do you feel sluggish and tired all day long? Exercising more and changing your diet, and still the weight will not come off? Told it is a part of life; everyone goes through it. Or my labs are normal, so why do I still feel this way? Did you know that hormone levels in both men and women can begin to decline as early as age 35? With the change in our hormone levels this leads to a variety of unpleasant symptoms (in both men & women) that include weight gain, memory loss, fatigue, depression, anxiety, increased belly fat, hair loss, and insomnia.

    Hormones are powerful chemicals produced by your endocrine glands. Their job is to communicate messages from your brain that regulate essential bodily functions, including:

    • Mood
    • Reproduction
    • Libido (sex drive)
    • Physical growth and development
    • Male and female sex characteristics
    • Metabolism- how your body gets energy from the foods you eat

    To work correctly, hormones need to be present at the right levels. Any kind of imbalance, whether it’s too much or too little, can have a significant effect on your health and mood. Hormone replacement therapy (HRT) can help rebalance the hormones in your body to restore normal function. Here, we use bio-identical hormone replacement therapy.

    Bio-identical hormones are derived from plants. They have exactly the same chemical and molecular structure as hormones that are produced in the human body.  Bio-identical hormones include Estradiol (E2), Estriol (E3), Progesterone, Testosterone, Dehydroepiandrosterone (DHEA), and Pregnenolone, and Thyroid.  Bioidentical hormone prescription is custom compounded in specific pharmacies in various preparations such as oral capsules, sublingual troches or tabs, transdermal cream or gel, and vaginal or rectal suppositories.  Bio-identical hormones can relieve the symptoms associated with declining hormones. A supervised treatment plan using natural hormone replacement can make a person feel healthier and younger.

    Synthetic vs Bioidentical Hormones

    Conventional hormone replacement uses estrogens derived from the urine of pregnant horses and conventional progestin is a synthetic hormone. Since the chemical composition of these conventional hormones is different from the hormones produced naturally, they remain structurally foreign to the human body. This is the major cause of several undesirable side effects. These are also the hormones referred to in the large Women’s Health Initiative Study.

    Other benefits of bio-identical hormone replacement therapy are:

    • Improve libido
    • Decreased body fat
    • Increased muscle mass
    •  Improve sleep and better mood
    • Better concentration and cognitive function
    • Increased bone density/reduce risk of osteoporosis

    IV Nutritional Therapy

    IV Nutritional Therapy has been used for decades to deliver directly into the veins of the patient a sterile solution of vitamins, minerals, and micronutrients to the body that cannot be achieved by oral supplementation.

    IV supplementation can benefit you if you’re:

    • Fatigued
    • Dehydrated
    • Nutrient-deficient
    • Trying to lose weight
    • Sick or immunocompromised
    • Performing below your ability 
    • Recovering from a tough workout
    • Prone to headaches and migraines
  • Chronic illnesses (chronic fatigue syndrome, auto-immune disorders, arthritis, Lyme’s disease)
    • Our IV therapies contain the highest quality compounded natural vitamin products at the safest and highest concentration with the most reasonable pricing.

    IV nutrition therapy bypasses digestion to provide quicker delivery to the cells that need it to replenish deficiencies or aid in healing. Often, orally ingested vitamins are passed through the stomach and excreted in the urine without any beneficial use at all. The benefit of IV nutritional therapy is to improve the body’s state of health and overall well-being, boosting immune function, decrease inflammation, and prevent dehydration.
