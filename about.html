    • About Us

    Armed with a Bachelor in Science of Biology from Xavier University of Louisiana, Ms. Fatmata KaiKai decided to pursue a career in medicine. 

    In 2007, as a graduate from Mercy College Physician Assistant Program in Bronx, New York, she worked in various specialties of medicines such as obstetrics & gynecology, pain management, and correctional medicine. Through her experiences, she found her niche in community health where she enjoyed educating the patients in the community to a healthier lifestyle. 

    After 15 years of practicing medicine, Ms.KaiKai decided upon integrative medicine. Integrative medicine is an approach to medical care that recognizes the benefit of combining conventional (standard) therapies (such as drugs and surgery) with complementary therapies (such as acupuncture, yoga, dietary supplements) that have been shown to be safe and effective.

    Integrative medicine is a prevention, lifestyle, and healing-orientation medicine that takes account of the whole person. This includes all aspects of lifestyle to improve the quality of life and emphasizes the therapeutic relationship between practitioner and patient. This was the foundation and mantra for her next chapter-EEU, integrative medicine & aesthetics clinic. Part of the EEU family since inception where her mission is to promote healthier lifestyle to lower the risk of certain age- related conditions and encourage the patient to be a part of the process. In her spare time, she commits her time to volunteering, reading, and traveling with family and friends.
