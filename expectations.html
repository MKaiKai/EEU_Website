    Visit Information

    IV Nutritional Therapy

    • At a consultation, your clinician can help you go over each of the available IV therapy options. They make recommendations according to your needs at the time of your visit, if you’re not already sure which option to choose.
    • Receiving an IV can take from 45 minutes to 2 hours, depending on treatment. You should be well hydrated and have eaten a full meal prior to the treatment
    • During the process, you get to sit comfortably as the nutrients passively flow into your vein.
    • You are monitored the entire time by a licensed medical provider for safety, but you’re free to read, browse your phone, or listen to music during your IV therapy sessions. 

    Bio-identical Hormone Replacement [BHRT]

    • At the initial consultation, a comprehensive review of your health history, order comprehensive labs that include thyroid panel, cholesterol panel, metabolic panel, complete blood count, hormone levels, and go over the BHRT treatment plan.
    • Follow-up in 2-4 weeks: review of blood hormone levels plus initiation of therapy. Adjustment of treatment accordingly.
    • Follow up in 3-6 months: review hormone labs and adjust your therapy accordingly. Further follow ups will be discussed at that time.
    Medical Weight Loss
    • At a consultation, a comprehensive health evaluation including review of your health history, EKG, labs, a blood pressure check, body composition analysis, and lifestyle modifications. All of which will be thoroughly explained to you in detail during the visit.
    • Follow-up in 4 weeks: a blood pressure check, a body composition analysis, lifestyle modifications, and refill of weight loss medication.
    • Weight program includes medication-Semaglutide, Lipotropics, oral medications- individually tailored to patients needs and goals.
    • Weekly LipoB injections are available and optional at an additional cost.
